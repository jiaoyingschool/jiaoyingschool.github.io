Principal Jiao Ying Awarded Youth Art  Education Teacher Achievement Award 

焦莹校长获儿童美学教育成就奖



On November 28 2008, Principal Jiao Ying was awarded Youth Art Education Teacher 
Achievement Award by the World Association of Chinese Artists in honor of her 
contribution to promote and develop children art education. Every year, students from 
Jiaoying Chinese and Art School are awarded in all kinds of art contests for their 
artwork achievements. Many of their artwork are collected by US Children Art Museum 
and exhibited in annual world children artwork shows. 

Mr. Qige Guan, the chairman of the World Association of Chinese Artists presented the 
certificate to Principal Jiao Ying. 
   
The World Association of Chinese Artists also appointed Principal Jiao Ying a standing 
counselor of the World Association of Chinese Artists

11月28日，国际中国美术家协会常务会长管其格先生，为奥勒州中华文化艺术学校校长焦莹颁奖，
授予焦莹校长儿童美学教育成就奖。鉴于此校学生多年来在历届美术创作绘画比赛中取得多项大奖，
每年均有学生作品被美国儿童艺术博物馆收藏并参加每年的世界儿童艺术绘画巡回展览。为研究
推动和发展儿童美术教育作出了贡献。

管其格先生代表协会聘任焦莹女士为国际中国美术家协会的常务理事，并颁发了聘任证书。
