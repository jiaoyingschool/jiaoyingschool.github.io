Academic >>Chinese Dance

　

We offer dance classes from beginning level through intermediate level to advanced level. The courses emphasize on dance alignment, principle of movement, development of individual skills. 

The benefits: Physical, mental development, improved coordination and concentration. Courses are designed to introduce kids to the love music and creative movement, and choice to for more formal dance/movement classes. Focus is on enjoyment! 

Uniforms: Appropriate dance-wear for practice (check with the teacher.) Our school will provide formal dance wear for stage performing occasion.
