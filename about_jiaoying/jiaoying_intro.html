  • Founder and Principal of Jiao Ying Chinese Culture and Art School in Portland Oregon
  • Outstanding Teacher by US Children Art Center and Oregon Humane Society
  • 21st century Art Contribution Award from the US Asian Pacific Art Academy
  • Outstanding Overseas Teacher Award from State Council of Chins
Jiao Ying Chinese Culture and Art School  焦莹中华文化艺术学校
  • Founded in 1997 by Ms. Jiao Ying
  • Fully private owned
  • Currently located at the campus of Portland State University in beautiful down town Portland Oregon
  • School principal: Ms. Jiao Ying
  • Over 160 pieces have been awarded since 2001, here are some hights
    • 1st place in group K-2, "Wonderful, Colorful Contest 2008"
    • 1st place in group 6-8, "Wonderful, Colorful Contest 2007"
    • 1st place, "Federal Junior Duck Stamp Art Contest 2006"
    • 2nd place, "Drawing the Line on Gun Violence" National Poster Contest 2002
    • 3 students' oil pastel artworks have been collected by World Children Art Museum since 2002
  • Art work from Jiao Ying School are well accepted by local arts galleries, including arts Gallery of Portland State University and Gottlieb Gallery
  • In addition, students from dancing and martial arts classes are frequently involved and invited to perform in local events
  • Years of effort has made Jiao Ying Chinese Culture and Art School a well-known culture entity in the Portland area. Children of many families learn their first Chinese character from the school
