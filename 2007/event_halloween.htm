Halloween Party     

With the help of PTA, our school holds the annual Halloween  trick-or-treat party and  costume competition.  This event has become our kids' favorite event of the year. they dress up like whatever they want to be and have tons of fun. What is more, they can win a prize for doing so !   Our PTA members will help to pick the winners -- the cutest costume, the most creative costume, the scariest costume, and the prettiest costume.    

Happy Halloween !
