Summer Picnic Party   

It is a beautiful sunny day, students and their family enjoy the fresh air and beautiful outdoor view as they share the food every family brings to this annual outdoor potluck party. The kids will give some dance/martial art performance after lunch, and play all kind of fun games. while parents are busying watching the show and socializing, Best Food Prizes will be given to the most popular salad, main course and dessert !
