A001

Preliminary Drawing Class

4 year-old +

None

Learn basics of line, shape and form and create beautiful work of art using oil  pastel. Develop skills of distinguishing shapes as well as  improving  senses of color.

A002

Intermediate Drawing Class

6 year-old +

A001

Introduction to composition, color theory and painting style. Focus on animal drawing.  Students will learn how to express their own creativity while develop understanding of perspective, movement and observation.

A003

Advance Drawing

8 year-old +

A002

With solid foundation in drawing, student will unleash their imagination to create strong art work and develop their own style with a final masterpiece.

A005

Cartoon Drawing class

4 year-old +

None

This course teaches cartoon character design, as well as skills of modeling, ratio and color. kids work in creative studio atmosphere.

A006

Traditional Chinese Drawing class

7 year-old +

Chinese painting background needed

Teaches traditional Chinese painting of flower, bird, fish and insect on Chinese rice paper. focus on composition and brushworks.

D001

Beginner Dance class

4 year-old +

None

Offers instructions to basic position, steps and movement used in rhyme, dance and ballet. kids will produce and perform simple group dance play for families and friends.

D002

Intermediate Dance Class

6 year-old +

D001

Offers instructions to foundation of Chinese dancing include posture, musicality, walk and vocabulary of steps while producing and performing a group dance play. Provides public stage performance opportunities.

D003

Youth Dance Class

8 year-old +

D002

This course will enhance students' skill of style, rhythm, timing and connection with other dancers while producing and performing a group dance play. Provides public stage performance opportunities.

M001

Beginner Martial Art class

6 year-old +

None

Focus on basic poise, punches, kicks and coordination.

M002

Intermediate Martial Art class

7 year-old +

M001

Focus on full sets of martial art movement.

C001

Pinyin

4 year-old +

None

Offers instructions in Chinese PinYin, the key to  Chinese character pronunciation.

C002

Chinese I

C001

Teaches simple Chinese characters/phases, start building vocabulary. Emphasize on active communication of listening, speaking, reading, and correct pronunciation

C003

C006

Chinese V

C110

Chinese Opera Class

10 year-old + frequent

speaker

Student will cast as a role in a  traditional Chinese opera to promote their interests and  to help them better understanding Chinese culture and history.

CS01

StandFord Chinese Class

(1st grade)

6 year-old + Mandarin Speaking Family

Use the famous Standford Chinese material (part 1), focus on fun Chinese speaking and building large vocabulary.

CS02

Standford Chinese Class (2nd grade)

8 year-old + Mandarin Speaking Family

CH01

Happy Chinese Class （A)

3.5 year-old +

None

Focus on speaking, reciting and reading thru nursery rhyme/poem, combines with fun activities/games  such as dance, drawing and performing to improve student's interest. Emphasize on correct pronunciation. (no writing)

CH02

Happy Chinese Class(B)

6 year-old +

None

Focus on speaking, reciting and reading thru nursery rhyme, combined with fun activities/games such as  dance, drawing and performing to improve student's interest. Emphasize on correct pronunciation. (no writing)
