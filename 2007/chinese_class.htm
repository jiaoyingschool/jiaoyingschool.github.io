  New promotion policies on Chinese language enrichment program

In order to encourage our students in better mastering Chinese language, Our school determines to enforce a new set of policies to inspire our kids' enthusiasm and interest, in addition to hold annual Chinese Speech Contest & Chinese Composition Contest during Christmas season for each age group, we will now publish and post  the best compositions online.

To further encourage our students to excel at Chinese studies, our school has decided to award student winners and assisting teachers  from our designated Chinese Contests  or any students who have their Chinese papers/journals published on local papers.

Here is the highlights of our new policies:

For our school designated Chinese contests,  we will award

      $100 for Grand place student winner and $100 to assisting teacher

      $80   for first place student  winner and $80 to assisting teacher

      $60   for second place student winner and $60 to assisting teacher

      $40   for third place student winner  and $40 to assisting teacher

For any of our students whose Chinese compositions are accepted and published on World's Daily newspaper or People's daily newspaper or local newspaper.

     we will award $5 a piece to student author.

 

  教材 Textbook

本校中文教学以教授简体字为主, 采用三套不同教材.

Our Chinese enrichment programs teach only simple case Chinese character. and use the following three different sets of Chinese textbooks & materials.

《马立平海外华裔儿童中文教材》:  Standford textbook

      针对普通话家庭背景的学生, 采用直接认字法--直接认字，后学拼音. 对中文听、说、读的要求比对写的要求高,

认字和阅读教学为主，让学生掌握大量的语汇. 斯坦福教材并配有CD，以提高学生做作业的兴趣. 具体教材采用说明, 请

参阅 《马立平海外华裔儿童中文教材》网址 :  http://www.mychineseschool.com

Standford textbook is designed for students from mandarin-speaking family background. It starts with recognizing and memorizing common characters, followed by teaching pinyin (Chinese phonics) . it emphasizes on hearing, speaking and reading rather than writing. Lots reading and vocabulary building activities involved.

This set of textbook comes with CD ROM for review and homework.

《 暨南大学教材1－6册》:  Chinese textbook from Jinan university

     针对感兴趣学习中文的学生, 没有中文背景和不会简单的日常中文会话的学生also fine , 先教拼音, 后学汉字. 要求 “听、说、读、写”四会并进.        

This is the traditional Chinese textbook widely used in Chinese school across US. it is targeted for both students with and without Chinese background. it starts from teaching pinyin, followed by recognizing Chinese characters. it emphasizes equally on hearing, speaking, reading and writing.

 

《 快乐汉语 》:  Happychinese textbook

     本校中文教学试点教材, 为宗合课，以中文为主导容入美术，舞蹈，唱歌等内容。意在寓教于乐使学生兴趣盈然，迅速提高。并启发及培养学生的特殊才能和天赋. 希望它能帮助我们的孩子更有兴趣、更有信心、更有效地在美国学习中文.

This is the experimental material which combines Chinese language learning with Chinese art, dance and singing in order to boost the interest of language learning.
