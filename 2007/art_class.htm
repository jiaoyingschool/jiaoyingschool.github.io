  师资 teacher introduction

 

本校中文教学聘有3位双语资深美术教师.

Ms.Jiao, Yin

Our principle, Ms. Jiao, is a senior education specialist herself. She was honored as Outstanding Teacher by US Children Art Center and Oregon Humane Society. She received the 21st Century Art Contribution Award from the US Asian Pacific Art Academy. China State Council honored her as an Outstanding Overseas Teacher.

Ms. Zhang, Li

Studied in China Beijing Art School and College and worked at a Repertory Theater group. since 1976, She has been involved in costume design and puppet making in the Beijing China Puppet Theater. She designed costumes and puppets in over 100 shows and also for CCTV, Singapore, Japan and USA. In 1996 She moved to Australia and taught Chinese dancing and art. In 2000, She moved to Oregon.

In addition to art, she also teaches dance.
 

从六岁起曾在战友杂技团学员班，北京舞蹈学院，北京空政文工团学习工作。1976年调入中国木偶剧团从事美术工作，同期进入中央工艺美术学院学习，在这期间我设计并制做百部以上的木偶及电视连续剧。我的美术作品曾在中国，日本，新加坡，美国各美术馆及电视台展出和销售。

1996年移民澳大利亚从事美术舞蹈教学工作.2000年来到美国继续教学.

Ms. Chen, Ella

Over eleven years of experience working in the art field. B.A and A.A. degrees in Fine Art. Expertise in oil based painting, watercolor, and portraits.

Taught art classes to middle and high school students, 50 students at a time with a total of 250 students each week. using oil based painting, watercolor, and portraits using pencils.

Her oil painting masterpieces, primarily landscape and flowers were sold by galleries and displayed in art exhibits in California and Atlantic City, New Jersey.
